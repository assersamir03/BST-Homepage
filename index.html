BSTU

The Republican Decree to establish Beni Suef Technological University was issued on June 3, 2019, regarding the issuance of Law No. 72 of 2019 establishing technological universities. This step is an important step in the development of a new path for technical education in Egypt.

About Us

Beni Suef Technological University is dedicated to advancing technical education in Egypt. Our institution focuses on providing innovative programs and international collaboration to prepare students for success in various technology-driven fields. Below are the key highlights of our university:

Establishment

Founded through Republican Decree on June 3, 2019, marking a significant milestone in Egypt’s technical education sector.

Degrees Granted

Professional diplomas, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees in technology across various specializations.

Programs Offered

Specialized programs in mechatronics, ICT, autotronics, and renewable energy designed to meet market demands.

International Collaboration

Strong partnerships with South Korea's KOICA, Korea Tech, and ABEEK Foundation for quality assurance and support.

Facilities and Curriculum

State-of-the-art facilities and programs tailored to align with national projects and geographical considerations.

Student Admission

Acceptance criteria include technical and general secondary education certificates, ability tests, and medical examinations.

BST

Beni Suef Technological University (BSTU) is collaborating with KOICA to enhance technical education in Egypt. This partnership focuses on introducing innovative programs in fields like mechatronics, ICT, and renewable energy, aiming to align education with market demands. The initiative includes equipping students with cutting-edge technology and fostering a culture of practical learning.

By 2028, the collaboration intends to significantly boost educational capacity and empower the local workforce. This six-year plan emphasizes skill development, inclusivity, and creating employment opportunities for Egyptian youth.
