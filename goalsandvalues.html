Our Mission

To provide high-quality technological education that prepares students for the demands of the modern workforce while fostering innovation and sustainable development in Egypt.

Strategic Goals

  • Establish an integrated path for applied and technological education parallel to academic education
  • Implement technology for societal benefit and qualify graduates to meet labor market needs
  • Provide high-quality educational services comparable to global standards
  • Prepare technologists capable of continuous learning and career flexibility
  • Establish strategic partnerships with educational institutions
  • Foster public-private sector participation in technical education
  • Provide technical and administrative consulting services
  • Join international educational organizations and bodies
  • Contribute to educational policy development
  • Support institutions through consultations and technical assistance
  • Conduct labor market research and needs assessment
  • Enable graduates to compete in global job markets

Core Values
