International Cooperation & Partnerships

Beni Suef Technological University strives to establish numerous cooperation protocols with various organizations and institutions to enhance joint cooperation and advance technical and technological education.

Benefits of Our Partnerships

Innovation & Research

Access to cutting-edge research facilities and collaborative innovation opportunities

Student Development

Enhanced learning opportunities and practical industry experience

Career Opportunities

Direct pathways to employment and industry connections

Higher Technological Institute Protocol

Focuses on providing educational and technological guidance services, developing faculty capabilities, organizing joint conferences, and encouraging collaborative research.

Egypt Workers Association Protocol

Collaboration in employment, training, and professional qualification for students across various specializations, supporting distinguished graduation projects.

Military Technical College Protocol

Aims to qualify military officers with technological bachelor's degrees and facilitate exchange of expertise between institutions.

Nuclear Materials Authority Protocol

Focuses on research collaboration, technical training, and development of professional certifications in various specialized fields.

Samsung Cooperation Agreement

Provides students with experimental educational opportunities in ICT and mechatronics, including professional training for competitive advantage.

Egyptian Space Agency Protocol

Collaboration in space technology research, specialized laboratory training, and development of joint technology programs.
