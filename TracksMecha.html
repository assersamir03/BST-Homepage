Master Mechatronic Systems

Our Mechatronics program integrates mechanical engineering, electronics, and smart automation. Students gain expertise in industrial robotics, PLC programming, and embedded systems design through hands-on projects with industry-grade equipment.

Pioneer Industrial Automation

Specialize in advanced manufacturing technologies including CNC systems, IoT-enabled machinery, and AI-driven process control. Develop skills in pneumatic systems, hydraulic controls, and digital twin simulations.

Expert Faculty

Learn from certified automation engineers with industry experience in robotics integration and smart manufacturing systems.

Industrial Lab Training

Hands-on practice with FANUC robots, Allen-Bradley PLCs, and 3D printing systems in our advanced automation workshop.

Technical Resources

Access industrial automation manuals, CAD/CAM software licenses, and mechatronic simulation platforms.

Industry Certifications

Earn credentials aligned with Siemens Mechatronic Systems Certification and FANUC Robotics certifications.
